## 🤎☕ About Us

We are a tiny independent coffee shop in the middle of the city. We roast our own beans every Tuesday and Friday. We don't do avocado toast that costs $18. We do good coffee, good cookies, and a quiet place to sit.

No loyalty cards. No upsells. Just coffee.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/73097560/115834477-dbab4500-a447-11eb-908a-139a6edaec5c.gif">


## ✨ Signature Features

- ☕︎  **Interactive Drink Customizer** — Select roast, milk ratio, sweetness, and toppings with live visual feedback.
- 🥐 **Artisanal Bakery Pairing** — AI-driven pastry pairings based on your coffee choice.
- 💳 **Speedy Pour Checkout** — Fast, encrypted payments via Stripe & Apple Pay.
- 🪑 **Cozy Seat Reservations** — Pick your spot by the window or near a charging outlet.
- 🌙 **Warm Latte Dark Mode** — Designed with eye-comfort warm sepia & mocha tones.

> *"Life's too short for bad coffee... and bad websites."* ☕✨
